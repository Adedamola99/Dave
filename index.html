The Face Behind The Look.

Dave Akpan is a Lagos-based fashion model whose commanding presence and innate sense of style have made him a sought-after name across editorial, runway, and commercial campaigns throughout West Africa and beyond.

With over five years in the industry, Dave blends raw authenticity with refined elegance—adapting effortlessly to every creative direction from minimalist luxury to bold avant-garde concepts.
