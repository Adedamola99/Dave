01 / About

The Face Behind The Look.

David Akpan is a Lagos-based model known for his commanding presence, versatility, and refined sense of style. Over the past five years, he has built a strong portfolio across fashion, editorial, lifestyle, and commercial campaigns, collaborating with leading brands and creatives.

Working professionally as Theblacdave, David brings a unique balance of raw authenticity and modern elegance to every project—adapting seamlessly to minimalist luxury, contemporary menswear, or bold avant-garde concepts.
